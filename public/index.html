Bharat

I’m a big fan of blues music because of the majestic history of the genre and the stories of all the great musicians who have played it. 2 years ago I decided to buy a guitar and teach myself to play blues like my favourite artists. And I got decent at playing the guitar but later on I realised that my improvement has plateaued and I needed some professional support. That is when I found Harshad and his amazing music school.Harshad has a great ear for music and an engaging style of teaching. On the first day he made me realise the very important difference between playing the guitar and playing MUSIC on the guitar. He then took the effort to introspect and identify a coaching program that is unique to me. I have been learning from him for about 2 months now and it has made a huge impact on the way I listen to music and find those notes and melodies on the guitar. As he says, it is all in building the ear.I have to say that learning with Harshad is a vibe. He has the coolest studio with a wonderful outdoor sitting and I can jam out for as long as I want. He also coaches many other students, who are a few years younger to me, but are way ahead in their understanding of music and their training of the listening.If you want to play an instrument for the first time or improve your technique, then don’t hesitate and call Harshad.
